Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 July 2026

This policy covers the OrionCircle applications used by the operator of the Etsy shop OrionCircle to publish their own product content to their own accounts (e.g. Pinterest).

Who this is for

These applications are private and single-user. They are used only by the shop owner to post their own marketing content. There is no sign-up, and no third party can authenticate with them.

What data is accessed

The applications access only the operator's own connected accounts, in order to publish the operator's own content (for example, creating pins on the operator's own Pinterest boards and reading those boards so content is filed correctly).

What data is stored

On a private server controlled by the operator: OAuth access/refresh tokens for the operator's own accounts, and the IDs of the operator's own boards and published content. These are readable only by the operator's own system account and are used solely to publish content.

No data belonging to any other user is collected, stored, processed, or shared. There is no analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party data sharing.

Cookies

These applications have no website front-end and set no cookies.

Data sharing

No data is shared, sold, or transferred to third parties. Content published to a platform is, by nature, publicly visible on that platform — that is the purpose of the applications.

Retention & deletion

Tokens are retained only while an application is in use. The operator can revoke access at any time from the relevant platform's account settings, which immediately invalidates the tokens. To request deletion of stored tokens, contact the address below.

Children

These applications are not directed at children and collect no data from anyone other than the operator.

Updates to this policy will be published at this URL with a new date.
