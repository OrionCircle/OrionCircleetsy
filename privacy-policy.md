# Privacy Policy — OrionCircle

**Last updated: 11 July 2026**

This policy covers the OrionCircle Pinterest application ("the app"), a private tool used by
the operator of the Etsy shop [OrionCircle](https://www.etsy.com/shop/OrionCircle) to publish
their own product images to their own Pinterest account.

## Who this app is for

The app is **private and single-user**. It is used only by the shop owner to post their own
marketing content. It is not offered to other users, has no sign-up, and no third party can
authenticate with it.

## What data the app accesses

The app requests access to the operator's own Pinterest account in order to:

- create pins on boards owned by the operator
- read the operator's own boards, so pins are filed in the right place

## What data the app collects and stores

The app stores, on a private server controlled by the operator:

- an OAuth access token and refresh token for the operator's own Pinterest account
- the IDs of the operator's own boards and the pins the app has created

These are stored in a file readable only by the operator's own system account. They are used
solely to publish the operator's content.

**The app does not collect, store, process, or transmit any data belonging to any other
Pinterest user.** It does not read other users' pins, boards, profiles, or activity. It has
no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party data sharing.

## Cookies and tracking

The app has no website and no user interface. It sets no cookies and performs no tracking.

## Data sharing

No data is shared with anyone. No data is sold. No data is transferred to third parties.
Content published to Pinterest is, by nature, publicly visible on Pinterest — that is the
purpose of the app.

## Data retention and deletion

Tokens are retained only while the app is in use. The operator can revoke the app's access at
any time from Pinterest account settings, which immediately invalidates the tokens. To request
deletion of stored tokens, contact the address below; because the app is single-user, this
simply means deleting the local credentials file.

## Children

The app is not directed at children and collects no data from anyone other than the operator.
