What the software does

The OrionCircle integration is a private, single-operator tool. It prepares short promotional videos and images for products the shop sells, and — with the shop owner's authorisation — uploads them to the shop's own connected accounts (for TikTok, into the account's Drafts/Inbox for the owner to review and post). It acts only on accounts the operator personally owns and has connected. It is not offered to third parties and has no other users.
